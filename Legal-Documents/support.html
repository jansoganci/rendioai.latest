Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Answer: Credits are the currency used to generate videos in RendioAI. The system works as follows:

  • 1 credit = 1 second of video generation
  • A 4-second video costs 4 credits
  • New users receive 10 free credits to get started
  • Credits can be purchased via Apple In-App Purchase
  • Credits never expire and are stored in your account

How do I request a refund?

Answer: All purchases are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. To request a refund:

  1. Visit Apple's Report a Problem page
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your RendioAI purchase
  4. Select "Request a Refund" and choose your reason
  5. Submit your request to Apple for review

Note: Credits are generally non-refundable according to our Terms of Service. However, if a technical error prevents video generation, we will automatically refund the credits used. For other refund requests, Apple will review on a case-by-case basis.

Why did my video generation fail?

Answer: Video generation may fail for several reasons:

  • Content Policy Violation: Your prompt may contain prohibited content (sexual, violent, copyrighted material, etc.)
  • Network Issues: Poor internet connection can interrupt the generation process
  • Server Overload: High demand may temporarily slow or interrupt service
  • Technical Error: Occasional AI model failures (rare but possible)

What happens to my credits? If generation fails due to our technical error, your credits are automatically refunded. If it fails due to a policy violation, credits are not refunded.

How long are my videos stored?

Answer: Generated videos are automatically stored for 7 days from the creation date. After 7 days, videos are permanently deleted from our servers. We recommend downloading important videos to your device before the 7-day period expires.

To download a video:

  1. Open the video in the History tab
  2. Tap the share/download icon
  3. Save to your device's Photo Library

Can I restore my credits on a new device?

Answer: It depends on your account type:

  • Guest Accounts: Credits are tied to your specific device and cannot be transferred. Guest accounts use device-based authentication.
  • Registered Accounts (Apple Sign-In): Once this feature is available, you'll be able to restore purchases across multiple devices using "Restore Purchases" in Settings.

Recommendation: When Apple Sign-In becomes available, upgrade your guest account to sync your credits across devices.

How do I delete my account and data?

Answer: To permanently delete your account:

  1. Open the RendioAI app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion

What gets deleted:

  • Your device ID and authentication data
  • All generated videos
  • Video generation history
  • Credit balance (unused credits are forfeited)

Warning: This action is irreversible and cannot be undone.

What content is prohibited?

Answer: RendioAI prohibits generating videos containing:

  • Sexual, pornographic, or nudity content
  • Excessive violence or gore
  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Illegal activities (drugs, weapons, crime)
  • Copyrighted or trademarked material without permission
  • Deepfakes or impersonation of real people
  • Child exploitation (strictly prohibited)

Violating our content policy may result in account suspension or termination. See our Terms of Service for details.

Can I use generated videos commercially?

Answer: Yes! You own the AI-generated videos created from your prompts. You have a perpetual, worldwide license to:

  • Use videos for personal or commercial purposes
  • Distribute videos on social media, websites, or other platforms
  • Monetize videos (e.g., YouTube ads, client projects)
  • Edit and modify videos as needed

Restrictions:

  • You cannot use videos for illegal purposes
  • You should not claim videos were created entirely by you without AI assistance
  • You cannot use videos to train competing AI models

Attribution: Not required, but appreciated (e.g., "Created with RendioAI").

Why is the app asking for permissions?

Answer: RendioAI may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library Access: Required to save generated videos to your device
  • Camera Access (if using image-to-video): Optional, to capture images for video generation

We do NOT request:

  • Location access
  • Contacts access
  • Microphone access
  • Tracking permissions

All permissions are optional. If you deny them, some features (like saving videos) may not work. See our Privacy Policy for more information.

The app is crashing or not working properly

Answer: Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Force close and restart the app
  2. Check your internet connection: AI video generation requires a stable connection
  3. Update to the latest version: Go to the App Store and check for updates
  4. Restart your device: Sometimes a simple reboot fixes issues
  5. Free up storage: Ensure you have at least 1GB of free space
  6. Reinstall the app: Delete and download RendioAI again (note: guest account data will be lost)

If the problem persists, contact us at umursoganci@gmail.com with:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • App version (found in Settings > About)
  • Description of the issue and when it occurs
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if possible

Troubleshooting Tips

Video generation is taking too long

  • Video generation typically takes 1-2 minutes
  • Complex prompts or high-resolution videos may take longer
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try switching between WiFi and cellular data
  • If it takes more than 5 minutes, cancel and try again

Video quality is not as expected

  • AI-generated videos are unpredictable and results vary
  • Try rephrasing your prompt with more specific details
  • Experiment with different themes and settings
  • Shorter prompts sometimes work better than long descriptions
  • Use the "Advanced Settings" to adjust resolution and aspect ratio

Credits were deducted but video failed

  • If the failure was due to our technical error, credits are automatically refunded
  • Check your credit balance in the Profile tab to confirm
  • If credits were not refunded and the error was on our end, contact umursoganci@gmail.com with your job ID
  • Note: Credits are not refunded if generation fails due to content policy violations

Cannot download or share videos

  • Grant Photo Library access: Settings > RendioAI > Photos > Allow Access
  • Ensure you have storage space on your device
  • Check that the video hasn't been auto-deleted (7-day limit)
  • Try force-closing the app and reopening
