## Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines prohibited content and behavior when using RendioAI. By using our Service, you agree to comply with this policy.

**Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or termination.**

## 1. Prohibited Content

You may NOT generate, upload, or share videos containing the following:

### 1.1 Illegal Content
- ❌ Content that violates any local, state, federal, or international law
- ❌ Illegal drugs, drug paraphernalia, or drug use
- ❌ Weapons, explosives, or instructions for making dangerous items
- ❌ Gambling, including illegal gambling promotions
- ❌ Fraudulent schemes, scams, or phishing
- ❌ Hacking, malware, or cyber attacks

### 1.2 Sexual and Adult Content
- ❌ Pornography or sexually explicit content
- ❌ Nudity or partial nudity
- ❌ Sexual acts or simulations
- ❌ Sexualized minors (strictly prohibited, zero tolerance)
- ❌ Sexual services or solicitation
- ❌ Fetish content

### 1.3 Violence and Gore
- ❌ Extreme violence, gore, or graphic injuries
- ❌ Torture, mutilation, or dismemberment
- ❌ Realistic depictions of death or suffering
- ❌ Animal cruelty or abuse
- ❌ Glorification of violence or mass shootings

### 1.4 Hate Speech and Harassment
- ❌ Content promoting hatred against protected groups (race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, etc.)
- ❌ Discriminatory or derogatory slurs
- ❌ Harassment, bullying, or threats
- ❌ Doxxing (sharing personal information without consent)
- ❌ Incitement to violence or harm

### 1.5 Child Safety (Zero Tolerance)
- ❌ Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- ❌ Sexualization of minors
- ❌ Content depicting child endangerment
- ❌ Grooming or predatory behavior

**CSAM is a serious crime. We report all instances to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and law enforcement.**

### 1.6 Harmful or Dangerous Content
- ❌ Self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
- ❌ Dangerous challenges or stunts
- ❌ Medical misinformation (e.g., fake cures)
- ❌ Conspiracy theories promoting harm
- ❌ Instructions for harmful activities

### 1.7 Misinformation and Manipulation
- ❌ Deepfakes of real people without consent (especially public figures)
- ❌ Impersonation or identity theft
- ❌ Election misinformation or voter suppression
- ❌ False claims intended to cause panic (e.g., fake emergencies)
- ❌ Synthetic media intended to deceive

### 1.8 Intellectual Property Violations
- ❌ Copyrighted material (movies, TV shows, music, books) without permission
- ❌ Trademarked content (brand logos, characters) without authorization
- ❌ Plagiarism or claiming others' work as your own
- ❌ Using someone's likeness without permission

### 1.9 Spam and Manipulation
- ❌ Spam, repetitive content, or flooding
- ❌ Clickbait or misleading titles
- ❌ Fake engagement schemes (bots, fake accounts)
- ❌ Pyramid schemes or multi-level marketing
- ❌ Phishing or social engineering

### 1.10 Privacy Violations
- ❌ Sharing others' personal information without consent
- ❌ Non-consensual intimate imagery (revenge porn)
- ❌ Stalking or invasive surveillance
- ❌ Unauthorized recording or footage

### 2.2 Account Misuse

- ❌ Exploiting bugs for financial gain

- ❌ Using the Service for commercial purposes without permission

### 2.4 Platform Manipulation

## 4. Enforcement

### 4.1 Automated Filters
We use automated safety filters to prevent prohibited content:
- Pre-generation: Prompts are scanned for prohibited keywords
- Post-generation: Videos are analyzed for harmful content
- Real-time: Rate limits prevent abuse

**Filters are not perfect.** Report any inappropriate content that bypasses filters.

### 4.2 User Reporting
Every video has a "Report" button. To report:
1. Tap "Report" on the video
2. Select reason (Sexual Content, Violence, Hate Speech, etc.)
3. Add optional description
4. Submit

**We review reports within 48 hours.**

### 4.3 Violations and Penalties

| Violation Severity | First Offense | Second Offense | Third Offense |
|--------------------|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Minor** (e.g., spam) | Warning | Content removal | 7-day suspension |
| **Moderate** (e.g., copyright) | Content removal + warning | 7-day suspension | Permanent ban |
| **Severe** (e.g., hate speech) | 7-day suspension | 30-day suspension | Permanent ban |
| **Critical** (e.g., CSAM) | **Immediate permanent ban** + **law enforcement report** | N/A | N/A |

**We reserve the right to skip warnings and ban immediately for severe violations.**

### 4.4 Appeals Process
If your content is removed or account is suspended:
1. Email **appeals@rendio.ai** with:
   - Your device ID or account email
   - Job ID of removed content
   - Explanation of why you believe this is an error
2. We review appeals within **7 business days**
3. Our decision is **final**

**Appeals are NOT available for:**
- CSAM violations (zero tolerance)
- Repeated violations
- Terms of Service violations (fraud, hacking)

## 5. Consequences of Violations

### 5.1 Content Removal
- Violating content will be deleted immediately
- You will NOT be refunded credits used for removed content
- Repeated violations lead to account action

### 5.2 Account Suspension
- **Temporary suspension:** 7 or 30 days (varies by severity)
- You cannot access the App during suspension
- Unused credits are forfeited (no refunds)
- Suspension history affects future penalties

### 5.3 Permanent Ban
- Account permanently disabled
- All data deleted
- No refunds for unused credits
- You are prohibited from creating new accounts

### 5.4 Legal Action
For severe violations, we may:
- Report to law enforcement (CSAM, threats, illegal content)
- Cooperate with legal investigations
- Pursue civil or criminal action
- Report to NCMEC (child safety violations)

## 7. Intellectual Property (Copyright/Trademark)

### 7.1 Copyright Infringement
If you believe content infringes your copyright:
1. Email **dmca@rendio.ai** with:
   - Your name and contact info
   - Description of copyrighted work
   - URL or job ID of infringing content
   - Statement of good faith belief
   - Statement under penalty of perjury
   - Your signature (digital)
2. We remove content within **24 hours** of valid DMCA notice
3. Repeat infringers have accounts terminated

### 7.2 Counter-Notice
If your content was removed and you believe it's a mistake:
1. Email **dmca@rendio.ai** with counter-notice
2. We forward to copyright owner
3. Content restored in 10-14 days unless owner files lawsuit

### 7.3 Trademark Violations
For trademark violations, email **legal@rendio.ai** with:
- Proof of trademark ownership
- Description of violation
- Requested action
