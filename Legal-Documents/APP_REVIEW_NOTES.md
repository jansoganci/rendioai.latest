## Non-Obvious Features Explained

### 1. Why Anonymous Authentication?
**Privacy-first design:**
- No email or password required
- No personal information collected
- DeviceCheck prevents abuse without tracking users
- Compliant with GDPR and privacy best practices

**How it works:**
- iOS generates a DeviceCheck token (cryptographic proof of device)
- Backend verifies token with Apple servers
- Backend creates anonymous Supabase auth session
- JWT token stored securely in iOS Keychain

---

### 2. Why Credit System Instead of Subscriptions?
**Fair pricing model:**
- Users pay only for what they use
- No recurring charges or commitments
- Transparent: 1 credit = 1 second (easy to understand)
- Credits never expire

**Prevents abuse:**
- Rate limiting: Max 10 videos per hour
- DeviceCheck ensures one free grant per device
- Atomic credit operations prevent double-charging

---

### 3. Why 7-Day Video Retention?
**Privacy and storage:**
- Reduces storage costs
- Limits data retention (GDPR-friendly)
- Encourages users to download important videos
- Automatic cleanup ensures compliance

**Future:** We plan to offer longer retention for paid tiers.

---

### 4. How We Prevent Inappropriate Content
**Multi-layer approach:**
1. **Pre-generation filtering:** Prompts scanned for prohibited keywords
2. **AI safety filters:** FalAI models have built-in content moderation
3. **User reporting:** Every video has "Report" button
4. **Manual review:** Reports reviewed within 48 hours
5. **Acceptable Use Policy:** Clear guidelines for users

**What's Prohibited:**
- Sexual/pornographic content
- Extreme violence or gore
- Hate speech or harassment
- Copyright/trademark infringement
- Illegal activities

See: Acceptable Use Policy at https://rendio.ai/acceptable-use

## Common Questions Anticipated

**Q: Why do you need DeviceCheck?**
A: To prevent credit fraud. Without it, users could reinstall the app repeatedly to get unlimited free credits. DeviceCheck allows us to verify "this device already received free credits" without collecting personal data.

**Q: Why is content moderation needed for AI-generated videos?**
A: Apple classifies AI-generated content as "User-Generated Content." Even though it's AI, users control what's generated (via prompts), so moderation is required to prevent abuse.

**Q: Can users generate inappropriate content?**
A: We use multiple layers of protection:
1. Prompt filtering (rejects prohibited keywords)
2. AI safety filters (built into FalAI models)
3. User reporting system
4. Manual review of reports
5. Acceptable Use Policy

**Q: What happens if generation fails?**
A: Credits are automatically refunded. Users see an error message with a "Retry" button. Failures are rare (<1%) and usually due to backend timeout or FalAI API issues.

**Q: Why 7-day video retention?**
A: Privacy and storage efficiency. Users can download videos they want to keep. This also complies with data minimization principles (GDPR). Future versions may offer longer retention for paid users.

**Q: Is data shared with third parties?**
A: Only for core functionality:
- FalAI: Receives prompts to generate videos (required for service)
- Apple: Handles IAP transactions (required for payments)
- Supabase: Backend infrastructure (required for data storage)

No data sold or shared for marketing/advertising.
