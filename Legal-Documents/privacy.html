Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Last Updated: January 16, 2025

Effective Date: January 16, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to RendioAI ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using RendioAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Device Identification

  • We generate and store a unique device identifier to enable anonymous authentication
  • This identifier is stored securely in your device's Keychain
  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information for guest users

Video Generation Requests

  • Text prompts you submit for video generation
  • Video settings (duration, resolution, aspect ratio)
  • Generated video files (stored temporarily)

Purchase Information

  • Credit purchase transactions processed through Apple In-App Purchase
  • We do NOT have access to your payment card details (handled by Apple)
  • We only receive a transaction ID to verify purchases

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

Usage Data

  • Video generation history (prompts, settings, timestamps)
  • Credit balance and transaction history
  • App interaction data (features used, session duration)

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version (iOS)
  • App version
  • Language and region settings

Technical Data

  • IP address (for API requests)
  • Error logs and crash reports (for debugging)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • ✗ Your real name or email (for guest users)
  • ✗ Contact lists or address book
  • ✗ Location data
  • ✗ Photos from your camera roll (unless you explicitly upload)
  • ✗ Browsing history or activity in other apps
  • ✗ Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • ✗ Cross-app or cross-site tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Provide Core Services

  • Process video generation requests
  • Manage your credit balance
  • Store and retrieve your video generation history
  • Authenticate your device for subsequent sessions

2.2 Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app
  • Debug technical issues and crashes
  • Optimize video generation performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.3 Process Payments

  • Verify In-App Purchase transactions with Apple
  • Grant credits upon successful purchase
  • Prevent duplicate charges and fraud
  • Maintain transaction records for accounting

2.4 Ensure Security & Safety

  • Detect and prevent abuse of the service
  • Monitor for prohibited content generation
  • Enforce rate limits to prevent spam
  • Maintain service integrity

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests and prevent harm
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

FalAI (Video Processing)

  • What we share: Your text prompts and video settings
  • Purpose: Generate videos using AI models
  • Data retention: Videos are processed and returned; FalAI does NOT store your videos long-term
  • Privacy Policy: https://fal.ai/privacy

Apple Inc. (Payments)

  • What we share: Purchase requests initiated by you
  • Purpose: Process In-App Purchases
  • Data handling: Apple handles all payment information; we only receive transaction verification
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)

  • What we share: All app data (device IDs, prompts, videos, transactions)
  • Purpose: Cloud database and storage
  • Data location: US East (Virginia)
  • Security: Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

3.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

  • Responding to court orders, subpoenas, or legal processes
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Protecting our rights, property, or safety
  • Preventing fraud or illegal activities

4. Data Retention

4.1 Video Files

  • Generated videos: Stored for 7 days after creation
  • Automatic deletion: Videos are permanently deleted after 7 days
  • User deletion: You can delete videos manually at any time

4.2 Transaction History

  • Credit transactions: Retained indefinitely for accounting and fraud prevention
  • Purchase receipts: Stored to enable "Restore Purchases" functionality

4.3 Account Data

  • Guest accounts: Device ID stored until you delete the app or request account deletion
  • Registered accounts (future): Stored until you delete your account

4.4 Logs and Analytics

  • Error logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated data retained for 1 year

5. Your Data Rights

5.1 Access Your Data

  • View your video generation history in the app (History tab)
  • View your credit balance and transaction history (Profile tab)

5.2 Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual videos: Swipe to delete in History tab
  • Delete all data: Use "Delete Account" in Profile > Settings
    • This removes: device ID, all videos, credit history, transaction logs
    • This action is irreversible

5.3 Data Portability

  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us at umursoganci@gmail.com
  • We will provide your data in JSON format within 30 days

5.4 Opt-Out of Data Collection

  • Since we use minimal data for core functionality, opting out means you cannot use the app
  • You may delete the app to stop all data collection

5.5 Correction of Data

  • Update your language and theme preferences in Profile > Settings
  • For other corrections, contact umursoganci@gmail.com

6. Data Security

6.1 Encryption

  • In transit: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • At rest: Database encrypted with AES-256
  • Device storage: Sensitive data stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)

6.2 Access Controls

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Backend uses service role keys stored securely (not in app code)
  • API keys never exposed to client

6.3 Anonymous Authentication

  • Guest users identified by device ID (not personal information)
  • No email or password required for basic usage
  • Reduces risk of credential theft

6.4 Incident Response

  • We monitor for security breaches
  • In case of a breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Contact: umursoganci@gmail.com
