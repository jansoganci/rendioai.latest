## Getting Started

### Q: Do I need to create an account?
**A:** No! RendioAI uses anonymous authentication. When you first open the app, a guest account is created automatically using Apple's DeviceCheck. No email or password required.

**Want a registered account?** Upgrade to Apple Sign-In (coming soon) to sync across devices.

---

### Q: How do I get started?
**A:** Simple! Just:
1. Open the app
2. You'll receive 10 FREE credits automatically
3. Tap any video model
4. Enter a text description
5. Tap "Generate Video"
6. Wait 40-90 seconds
7. Watch your AI-generated video!

---

### Q: What are the 10 free credits for?
**A:** New users get 10 free credits to try the service. Since 1 credit = 1 second of video, you can generate:
- Two 4-second videos (8 credits) + have 2 left
- One 8-second video + have 2 credits left
- Multiple shorter videos

**Note:** Free credits are granted once per device.

## Video Generation

### Q: How long does it take to generate a video?
**A:** Typically **40-90 seconds**, depending on:
- Video duration (longer videos take more time)
- Backend load (peak times may be slower)
- AI model complexity

**Tip:** Start with 4-second videos for faster results!

---

### Q: What makes a good prompt?
**A:** Great prompts are:
- **Descriptive:** "sunset over mountains with fog" (not just "sunset")
- **Specific:** Include details like colors, lighting, camera movement
- **Concise:** 10-30 words is ideal
- **Clear:** Avoid contradictory descriptions

**Examples of good prompts:**
- ‚úÖ "Ocean waves crashing on rocky shore at golden hour"
- ‚úÖ "Colorful geometric shapes morphing slowly, abstract art style"
- ‚úÖ "Forest path with sunlight streaming through trees, cinematic"

**Examples of poor prompts:**
- ‚ùå "Nice scenery" (too vague)
- ‚ùå "Cat playing piano while flying over New York" (too complex/contradictory)

---

### Q: Why did my video generation fail?
**A:** Failures are rare (<1%) but can happen due to:
- Backend timeout
- FalAI API error
- Network connectivity issue
- Prohibited content in prompt

**What happens:** You'll see an error message, and **credits are automatically refunded**.

**What to do:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a simpler prompt
3. Tap "Retry"
4. If it keeps failing, contact support@rendio.ai

---

### Q: Can I edit the video after generation?
**A:** Currently, no. RendioAI generates final videos that you can download and use. Editing features may be added in future updates.

**Workaround:** Download the video and use a third-party video editor (iMovie, etc.).

---

### Q: Can I regenerate a video with the same prompt?
**A:** Yes! In the History tab:
1. Tap any previous video
2. Tap "Regenerate"
3. The prompt and settings are pre-filled
4. Tap "Generate" again

**Note:** Each generation uses credits (even with the same prompt). AI may produce slightly different results each time.

---

### Q: What video formats are supported?
**A:** Videos are generated in **MP4 format** (H.264 codec), compatible with:
- iPhone/iPad Photos app
- Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- Most video editing software
- All major platforms

## Technical Issues

### Q: The app crashes when I tap "Generate." What do I do?
**A:** Try these steps:
1. **Force-close the app:** Swipe up from app switcher
2. **Reopen the app**
3. **Check iOS version:** Requires iOS 17.0+
4. **Update the app:** Check App Store for updates
5. **Restart your device**

**Still crashing?** Email support@rendio.ai with:
- iPhone model
- iOS version
- Description of what you were doing when it crashed

---

### Q: Video won't download to Photos. Help!
**A:** Check permissions:
1. Go to iPhone Settings
2. Scroll to "RendioAI"
3. Tap "Photos"
4. Select "Add Photos Only" or "All Photos"

**Still not working?** Try:
- Check storage space (Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí iPhone Storage)
- Restart the app
- Contact support if issue persists

---

### Q: App says "No internet connection" but I'm online. Why?
**A:** Possible causes:
1. **VPN or firewall:** Try disabling VPN temporarily
2. **Cellular restrictions:** Check Settings ‚Üí Cellular ‚Üí RendioAI (should be ON)
3. **Network issues:** Switch between WiFi and cellular
4. **Backend maintenance:** Check https://status.rendio.ai

**Quick fix:** Toggle airplane mode on/off, then reopen app.

---

### Q: Can I use RendioAI offline?
**A:** No, video generation requires an internet connection because:
- AI processing happens on cloud servers (computationally intensive)
- Videos are generated by FalAI's backend

**What works offline:**
- Viewing previously generated videos (if still in 7-day window)
- Accessing settings
